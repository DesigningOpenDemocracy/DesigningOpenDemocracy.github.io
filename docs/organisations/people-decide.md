People Decide (PD) was an Australian participatory democracy platform and associated political party. It aimed to give citizens a direct role in national legislation and local government decisions through an online voting platform.

The model combined a civic technology platform with electoral participation: citizens could propose issues, vote on policy positions, and contribute to law-making, while People Decide candidates in parliament would vote according to the platform's outcomes.
