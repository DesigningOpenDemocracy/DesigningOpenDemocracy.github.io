Liquid Democracy e.V. is a Berlin-based non-profit association founded in 2009 to develop free and open-source digital participation tools and support their deployment in governments, NGOs, and public institutions. The organisation combines software development with consulting, workshops, and policy research.

Their primary software, **Adhocracy+**, is a modular participation platform used by German federal and state institutions, NGOs, and international organisations. They also operate **meinBerlin** (mein.berlin.de), the official participatory platform for Berlin residents, through which Berliners submit ideas and take part in city planning decisions.

The organisation is distinct from the concept of liquid democracy — the name reflects an early focus, and their current work covers a broader range of digital participation approaches.
