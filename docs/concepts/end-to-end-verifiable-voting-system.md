title: End to End Verifiable Voting System
---

A voting system in which every voter can verify that their vote was recorded and counted correctly — without revealing how they voted — using cryptographic receipts and publicly auditable tallies. The goal is to provide mathematical proof of election integrity rather than relying solely on trust in administrators.

Research implementations and papers:

- [EVIV system paper](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/257006739_EVIV_An_end-to-end_verifiable_Internet_voting_system)
- [Helios voting protocol](https://eprint.iacr.org/2015/629.pdf)
- [EVIV code](https://github.com/EVIVoting/EVIV)
