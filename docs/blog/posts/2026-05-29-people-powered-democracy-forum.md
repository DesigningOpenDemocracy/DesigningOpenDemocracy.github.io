# Event: People Powered Democracy Forum — Carlton, 31 May

* **When:** Sunday 31 May 2026, 2:00 PM AEST
* **Where:** Kathleen Syme Library, Multi-Purpose Room 2 — 251 Faraday Street, Carlton VIC 3053
* **Organised by:** [Beyond Billionaires](mailto:movebeyondbillionaires@proton.me)

A forum on how citizens assemblies, sortition, and deliberative methods can restore democratic power to ordinary people.

<!-- more -->

## Speakers

- **Sonia Randhawa** (Sortition Foundation) — how citizens assemblies have been used around the world to break political deadlocks
- **Andrew George** (Remake the World) — lessons from the People's Assembly of Lismore, established after the 2022 floods
- **Jane Morton** (Beyond Billionaires) — campaign to redistribute wealth and fix democracy
- **Eddie Kowalski** (Conversations at the Crossroads) — large-scale deliberation on nuclear power using a Stanford University method; co-organiser of the recent Reclaiming Democracy event at Melbourne Town Hall
- **Craig Lambie** (Deliberative Super) — use of local deliberative processes to choose and guide independents

There will be plenty of time for questions and discussion.

## Attendance

This is an in-person event. If you can't attend in person, register anyway and a video recording will be sent to you. If you'd like to organise a viewing party in your local area, contact the organisers at [movebeyondbillionaires@proton.me](mailto:movebeyondbillionaires@proton.me).
